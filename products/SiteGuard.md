## Overview

SiteGuard is the next generation of site safety. For every $1 in direct site damage, $29 follows in hidden costs — schedule blowouts, legal liability, emergency contractors, insurance hikes, reputational damage. SiteGuard uses AI-powered oversight, geofencing, and real-time alerts to stop incidents before they happen.

In 2024 alone, the CGA DIRT Report documented **196,977 damage incidents** globally. SiteGuard was built to eliminate that number.

## The Problem SiteGuard Solves

### The $1:$29 Problem
- **$1** in direct damage (utility strike, machinery incident)
- **$29** in indirect costs:
  - Schedule extension rippling across the entire project
  - Legal liability and regulatory fines
  - Emergency contractor mobilisation at premium rates
  - Insurance premium increases at renewal
  - Reputational damage affecting the next bid

### The Scale
- 196,977 damage incidents documented in 2024 (CGA DIRT Report)
- Utility strikes are the #1 cause of unplanned project cost overruns
- Autonomous and semi-autonomous machinery is becoming standard on sites globally

## Core Features

- **Utility Strike Prevention** — AI-powered detection of proximity to underground services
- **Geofencing & Exclusion Zones** — Define no-go areas; machines receive real-time alerts
- **Autonomous Machine Monitoring** — Continuous oversight of autonomous and semi-autonomous plant
- **AI-Powered Safety Alerts** — Instant notifications before threshold breaches occur
- **Remote Override Capability** — Stop or redirect machinery remotely from dashboard
- **Global Compliance Ready** — Built to meet NZ, AU, and international safety standards
- **Incident Prevention Log** — Full audit trail of near-misses and interventions
