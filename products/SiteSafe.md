# SiteSafe — Product Specification

**Type:** SaaS Platform  
**Category:** Site Safety & Compliance Management  
**Status:** Active  
**Platform:** Mobile-first (iOS & Android) + Web Dashboard

---

## Overview

SiteSafe puts safety compliance in the hands of every team member on a civil construction site. From digital inductions to real-time hazard reporting, SiteSafe replaces paper-based processes with a fast, reliable mobile platform built for the realities of the job site.

---

## Core Features

- **Digital Site Inductions** — Replace paper inductions with mobile-friendly digital onboarding
- **Real-Time Hazard Reporting** — Workers log hazards instantly from the field
- **Toolbox Talk Management** — Schedule, deliver, and track toolbox talks digitally
- **Compliance Dashboards** — Live visibility of site compliance status for managers
- **Incident Tracking** — Log, track, and close out incidents in real time
- **Mobile-First Design** — Built for use on site, in gloves, in any weather
