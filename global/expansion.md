# Global Expansion Strategy — South Consultants

## Phase 1 — Home Market (Current)
- **New Zealand** — establish product-market fit with NZ civil contractors
- Key targets: Downer, Fulton Hogan, HEB Construction, Fletcher Infrastructure
- Leverage NZ construction industry networks

## Phase 2 — Australia (2026)
- Direct SaaS expansion — no physical office required
- Target utility construction and infrastructure contractors
- Align with Australian WHS legislation requirements
- Key bodies: Safe Work Australia, AICLA

## Phase 3 — Southeast Asia (2027)
- Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia — high infrastructure investment
- Partner with local construction industry associations
- Multilingual platform consideration

## Phase 4 — Middle East (2027-2028)
- UAE, Saudi Arabia — massive infrastructure pipeline (NEOM, Vision 2030)
- Target international EPC firms operating in the region
- Compliance alignment with local regulations

## Phase 5 — Global SaaS (2028+)
- Self-serve SaaS model for international markets
- English-first, multilingual roadmap
- API integrations with global construction platforms (Procore, Aconex, etc.)
