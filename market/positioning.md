## Core Positioning Statement

South Consultants is the only technology company purpose-built for civil construction that combines AI-powered site safety platforms, autonomous machinery oversight, and industry-certified training — from New Zealand to the world.

1. **Stop the $29** — For every $1 in site damage, $29 follows in hidden costs. Our platforms prevent that.

3. **AI-powered, human-controlled** — Autonomous oversight that keeps people informed and in control.
4. **Global ready** — Built in NZ, scaled for the world.
